About Us

A Joyful Space Where Little Minds Blossom

At Pebbles & Pine, we believe childhood is a beautiful journey filled with curiosity, creativity, and wonder. Our preschool provides a warm, nurturing, and joyful environment where children feel safe to explore, learn, and grow at their own pace.

Inspired by Montessori principles and nature-based learning, we encourage hands-on discovery, imaginative play, and meaningful experiences that help children build confidence, independence, and a lifelong love for learning.

At the heart of Pebbles & Pine is a simple belief — every child is unique, capable, and full of potential. Through gentle guidance, engaging activities, and caring educators, we help little minds blossom into confident learners and kind individuals.
